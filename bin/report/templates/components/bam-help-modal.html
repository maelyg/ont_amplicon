IGV user guide

The Integrative Genomics Viewer (IGV) is a powerful tool for visualizing genomic data. For more detailed instructions, please refer to the IGV User Guide.

Below are some tips to help you navigate and use IGV effectively:

  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out, and click and drag to move around the genome.
  • Click and drag along the scale bar to zoom into specific regions of the alignment.
  • If you aren't sure what a symbol in the alignment means, try clicking on it for more information.
